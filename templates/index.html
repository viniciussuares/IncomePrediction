Income Predictor

Objective

This form allows you to input sociodemographic variables to predict the monthly income of individuals in Brazil.

Limitations

  • This model was trained using only data from 2023.
  • The maximum salary during training was R$10,000 (97th percentile). Predictions above this are unreliable.
  • In general, the model accuracy decreases as the salary range increases.

Use the predictions as a guideline rather than a definitive answer.
